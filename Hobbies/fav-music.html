My Favourite Music

Gostra — Dzenking

About the song:
The song is about strong emotions, inner tension and personal struggles. It sounds tense and dramatic, as if the music conveys a sense of frustration and powerful emotions that cannot be hidden.

Atmosphere:
Dark, emotional, intense, and powerful.

White Night — Chernikovskaya Hata

About the song:
The song raises the topic of trust between people. It asks the question of whether a person really believes the other, and shows the fragility of human relationships.

Atmosphere:
Melancholic, nostalgic, calm, dreamy.

Do You Believe Me? — Lyudmila Berlinskaia

About the song:
The song raises the topic of trust between people. It asks the question of whether a person really believes the other, and shows the fragility of human relationships.

Atmosphere:
Emotional, thoughtful, intimate.

What Is Love? — Grad!ent

About the song:
The composition reflects on what love really means. It asks a philosophical question about feelings, relationships, and how people understand love.

Atmosphere:
Romantic, thoughtful, a little sad.

Toffees and Risks — Monetochka

About the song:
The song is about youth, choice and risk. It combines lightness, irony and unusual images through which thoughts about life and the courage to make decisions are conveyed.

Atmosphere:
Dark, emotional, intense, and powerful.
