Games

Mafia: The City of Lost Heaven

A classic crime story about a taxi driver Tommy Angelo, who accidentally finds himself drawn into the world of the mafia. The game is appreciated for its 1930s atmosphere, realistic city, and one of the strongest stories in games.

Mafia II

The story of Vito Scaletta and his path in a mafia family. Set in the 1940s and 1950s, the game is remembered for the atmosphere of post-war America, an excellent plot and the lively city of Empire Bay.

Mafia III

A darker story about war veteran Lincoln Clay, who takes revenge on the mafia for the destruction of his family. Set in 1968 in New Bordeaux, the game stands out for its serious theme, era style, and open world.
