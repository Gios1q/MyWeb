About me

Hi, I'm Maksym 👋

My name is Maksym Bondarenko. I was born on October 27, 2006, in Bilhorod-Dnistrovskyi, Odesa region.

I grew up in the village of Shabo — a calm and steady place where life moved at its own pace. I started school there when I was six and spent eleven years studying, making friends, and discovering what I cared about. Those years shaped my interests and the way I look at the world.

Because of the war, my family had to move to Ireland. I settled in Enniscorthy, a small town where everything felt new — language, culture, and school. Getting used to all that wasn’t easy, but it gave me a better sense of how to adapt and keep moving forward.

I continued my education at Enniscorthy Community College, where I completed my Leaving Certificate. Later, I applied to Waterford Further Education to keep learning and developing my skills.

Outside of studies, I’ve always been drawn to video games — not just as entertainment, but as a way to experience stories and emotions. The Mafia trilogy stands out for me because of its strong atmosphere and moral choices.

I’m also interested in philosophy — from Eastern ideas about balance and discipline to modern thoughts on society and human nature. I practice tea ceremonies to stay focused, and I draw to express ideas and moods visually. I enjoy Chinese animation and anime for their art styles and storytelling.

I collect figures, listen to music of different genres, play chess, and study the game of Go — I like how it rewards patience and long‑term thinking. I also watch films and series with an eye on story structure and character development.

I value discipline, thoughtful decisions, and the desire to keep improving. I respect people who are confident in their goals and dedicated to what truly matters to them.
